For 'Young' people only!, 26 January 2002

Author: jellyneckr

This is just one in the what seems like thousands of YOUNG INDIANA JONES movies that have been released direct-to-video during the past few years. This one takes place during the Great War and while some of the action scenes are fantastic, they can't make up for the hokey dialogue, annoying music, mediocre acting, and thin story line. Young people may enjoy it, but older people and fans of the YOUNG INDIANA JONES series will probably be disappointed.
