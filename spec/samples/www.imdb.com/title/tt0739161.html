How All Began, 6 September 2006

Author: arturo_smallcharm from USA

Best Mexican TV-Series, in this pilot we know the neighbors as they are, some of them are the desperate housewives, the unemployed husbands, the children which they are weirds, the young woman which is in search of the perfect man, the family where the parents make their son want to become a child movie star, even when the kid does not want to, among other amazing characters created by Gus Rodriguez and Eugenio Derbez.

In the pilot, we saw Silvia (Mayrin Villanueva) moving to the building, where she met her neighbors and these help her to move her stuffs to her new flat.

Magadalena and Arturo have one brainless and sloppy son Marco, and Alejandra a Marylin Manson wannabe.

Frankie is or was a movie actor on the movie La Toalla del Mojado, in which he spend all his time talking to the neighbors about the movie. His wife Lorena has been supporting him all her life. This couple has a son named Benito, the child star, even when he doesn't want to be one.

Don't miss this show, it's amazing.
