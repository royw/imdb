One of the best video games I have ever played, 11 July 2007

Author: hchais from United States

This game is just simply amazing. It is one of the best video games I have ever played. It was the second game I got for my PS2 when it came out in 2001. I have to admit that the graphics are pretty bad, but the rest of the game makes up for it. It is one of the longest games have ever played. It has about 60 levels. That is one long game. It is just so fun to play. The game is full of action, a really good story for video games, and is just so fun. From beginning to end, you become more interested in the game and you just want to finish it to see how it ends. If you loved, Raiders of the Lost Ark, Temple of Doom, and The Last Crusade, you will not be disappointed. I think that this is the second best video game I have ever played next to Mercenaries. This was a great game. I loved it. 10/10.
